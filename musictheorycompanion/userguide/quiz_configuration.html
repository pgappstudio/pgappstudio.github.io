Quiz Configuration

The Quiz Configuration page lets you configure the type of quiz you want to practice.

Music theory concepts are divided into following categories:

  • Note identification
  • Note reading
  • Interval identification
  • Interval comparison
  • Interval reading
  • Scale identification
  • Scale reading
  • Chord identification
  • Chord reading

On each category there are 6 levels (Level 1 - Level 6), level 1 being the easiest and level 6 the hardest.

Initially by randomly asking you questions from all the levels of all categories, the app learns about your current levels for each category.

And then the app asks you questions from all the categories based on your current level in any category.

As you continue improving your skills by practicing more, and your consistency of correct answer reaches a threshold level for any category, you will move to the next level in that category.

And the app will continue asking you questions from the next level.

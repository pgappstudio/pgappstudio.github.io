Home

Following musical tools are available in the Home screen:

  1. Composer: Compose your own songs with innovative chord progressions and lyrics
  2. Metronome: Very accurate and simple metronome with 60+ configurable percussion sounds, configurable time signatures, speed trainer
  3. Grand Piano: A realistic Grand Piano with high quality sounds of 20+ instruments
  4. Scales & Diatonic Chords: Shows how diatonic triads and seventh chords are formed for any scale
  5. Matching Diatonic Chords: Shows chord alternatives that can be played to accompany any note of any scale
  6. Chord Library: Collection of 1000+ chords with showing how chords are formed and named
  7. Matching Scales: Shows all possible scales that shares the chosen chords for any selected scale
  8. Circle of Fifth: Shows Circle-of-Fifths and Circle-of-Fourths for any scale
  9. Negative Harmony: Shows the Negative Harmony notes and chords for any selected scale and for the chromatic scale
  10. Multiple Scales: Tool helps composing using parallel scales, relative scales or totally unrelated scales. Helps borrowing chords from a different scale into your composition
  11. Cube Dance: A modern voice leading tool based on the Neo-Riemannian theory
  12. Modulations: Shows plenty of different chord progressions to smoothly change key from the selected source key to various destination keys
  13. Intervals: A two octave interval tool useful for ear-training. It also shows the feelings associated with each interval
  14. Scale Practice: Helps practicing any scale with voice or instrument
  15. Reference: Online references to Musical Symbols, Musical Terminologies and useful Music Theory concepts

Select any of the tools and then view the help in that tool page to know more about them.
